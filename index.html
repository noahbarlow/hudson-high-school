04Inside the program

A high school where
students still feel known.

No anonymity. No coasting. A small high school with serious academics, deeply human teachers, and pathways tailored to each student's ambition.

/ 01

Small by design

Classes capped at twenty. Teachers know each student's name, strengths, and stretch goals — and they teach to them.

/ 02

Subject specialists

Faculty with deep expertise in their disciplines, available outside class for one-on-one support, mentorship, and the conversations that actually shape a teen's thinking.

/ 03

Built for what's next

Every Grade 12 graduates with a postsecondary plan, supported by counsellors who know the universities — and your kid.

05The curriculum

Four years.
Earned, not endured.

Grade 09

Foundation

Core academics alongside introductory electives. Study skills and self-management built into the timetable.

English Principles of Math Science Geography Core French Health & PE
Grade 10

Exploration

Electives expand: visual art, instrumental music, advanced sciences, second languages. The first real taste of university-style learning.

Civics & Careers Canadian History Visual Arts Drama Computer Studies Spanish
Grade 11

Direction

Pathway shaping. University-prep courses. Dedicated counsellor sessions. Students start narrowing toward postsecondary destinations that fit who they are.

Functions (MCR3U) Biology Chemistry Physics English (NBE3U) World Religions
Grade 12

Launch

Capstone courses. Application strategy. Interview practice. Personal statements. Every student leaves with a place — and a plan they actually believe in.

Calculus & Vectors Advanced Functions English (ENG4U) Chemistry (SCH4U) Biology (SBI4U) Philosophy Data Management

07Your visit

A 30-minute tour.
Zero pressure.

/ 01

You arrive

Park on Ascot Ave or take the 512 streetcar to St. Clair West. We meet you at the front desk.

/ 02

You walk through

Classrooms in session. The library, the gym, the science labs. Real students mid-day, not staged.

/ 03

You meet our team

A short conversation with admissions and, when timing works, a high school teacher who can answer the specific questions you came with.

/ 04

You decide on your time

No pitch, no pressure. We follow up only if you ask us to. If it's a fit, we'll talk about applications next.
